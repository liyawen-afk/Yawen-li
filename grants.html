Active Grants

Measuring Dynamic Capability of Adult Day Health Services in the US

National Institute of General Medical Sciences (R16GM153550-01A1)
Role: Principal Investigator
Period: 2025–2029
Amount: $588,000

This project develops and validates measures of dynamic capability—the organizational capacity to sense, seize, and transform in response to environmental change—among adult day health services centers nationwide.

Binaural Beat Stimulation for Alzheimer's Disease

CSUSB Catalytic Investment on Research and Innovation Seed (CiRIS) Program
Role: Principal Investigator
Period: 2024–2025
Amount: $32,500

A community-based pilot study assessing the efficacy of music-based binaural beat stimulation for patients with Alzheimer's disease.
